## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account
Go to app.novasoft.io and click "Start Free". Enter your email and create a password. Verify your email within 24 hours.

### Step 2: Set Up Your Workspace
After login, you'll see the Setup Wizard. Enter your company name, invite team members by email, and choose your first project template (Scrum, Kanban, or Simple List).

### Step 3: Create Your First Project
Click "+ New Project" in the sidebar. Choose a template or start blank. Set project dates and assign a project lead.

### Step 4: Invite Your Team
Go to Settings > Team > Invite Members. Enter emails and set roles: Owner, Admin, Member, or Viewer.

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

### Login Problems
- Forgot password: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, check email within 5 minutes
- Account locked: After 5 failed attempts, account locks for 30 minutes
- SSO issues: Contact your IT admin to verify SAML configuration

### Billing Issues
- Payment failed: Update card in Settings > Billing > Payment Method
- Downgrade plan: Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan (takes effect next billing cycle)
- Cancel subscription: Settings > Billing > Cancel (data kept for 90 days)
- Request refund: Email billing@novasoft.io within 14 days of charge

### Performance Issues
- Slow loading: Clear browser cache, try incognito mode, check status.novasoft.io
- File upload failing: Max file size is 100MB. Check your storage quota in Settings > Storage
- Notifications not working: Check Settings > Notifications and browser permissions

### Data & Security
- Export your data: Settings > Account > Export Data (ZIP file emailed within 1 hour)
- Delete account: Settings > Account > Delete Account (irreversible after 30 days)
- Two-factor authentication: Settings > Security > Enable 2FA (strongly recommended)
- GDPR requests: Email privacy@novasoft.io

## API Documentation

### Authentication
All API calls require a Bearer token. Generate one at Settings > API > New Token.
Example: Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN_HERE

### Rate Limits
- Free: 1,000 requests/day
- Starter: 10,000 requests/day  
- Pro: 50,000 requests/day
- Enterprise: Unlimited

### Common Endpoints
- GET /api/v1/projects - List all projects
- POST /api/v1/projects - Create project
- GET /api/v1/tasks - List tasks
- POST /api/v1/tasks - Create task
- GET /api/v1/users/me - Get current user
